Welcome To KICUNGURO ANGEL CENTRE

Kicunguro Angel Centre is an initiative that seeks to give hop,purpose,self-esteem and empowerment to vulnerable teenage girls and older p[ersons through community development activities and privide appropriate shelter for pregnant teenagers who are homeless.

Get A Quote

Our Mission

To provide condusive environment to support teenage pregnant girls that enssure that they are safe and the newborn babies grow well, and the girls are eventually re-united to their families and schools to complete their education cycle.

Then for the older persons we help them to have a wholesome life in their aging cycle.

Our Vision

To create a world in which teenage pregnancy is no longer a hindrance to girl's empowerment and aging is no longer looked at as a disadvantage.

Statement of Need

In Sheema District as well as other parts of Western Uganda,immediately after COVID 19 lock down was enforced by the government and all students stopped going to school,an accelerated and alarming rate of Teenager pregnancy broke up like a COVID itself. Upon conception the girl is forced to go through a vicious cycle that pust her helath,eduaction,earning potential and her entire future in jeopardy.

She is forced to drop out of school to marry the man responsible for her pregnancy to take away the "shame" she is said to have brought to her family for becoming pregnant. The other options include but not limited to aborting the pregnancy which leads to trauma and all associated issues that come with abortion or marry any man not of her choice and thus end up misery all through her life. On the orther hand, the boy or man responsible for the pregnancy is left free and neither will he be held responsible for the care of the child and the mother.
